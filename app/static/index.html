About

Sentec DO is a BlueOS extension that reads a Sentec OXYnor dissolved-oxygen sensor over RS485 / Modbus RTU (via a BLUART adapter) and forwards readings to the autopilot's .BIN log as DO and TDO named-value floats.

Version: 0.1.0
